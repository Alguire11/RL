Goal: Track rent payments and build a credit report that can be shared with future landlords or lenders.

1. Onboarding & Account Creation

Lands on Welcome screen with tagline.

Clicks "Get Started"

Signs up via email/password or Google/Apple

2. Bank Connection

Sees brief explainer about Open Banking

Clicks "Connect My Bank"

Goes through mock connection or live Truelayer flow (MVP: simulate)

3. Set Rent Details

Inputs monthly rent amount

Sets recurring due date (e.g. 1st of every month)

Optionally adds landlord name/email

Optionally enters 3 past rent payments (manual entry)

4. Rent Tracking

App checks rent payment data every month (mock or live)

Displays rent streaks on dashboard

Shows next due date

Pushes reminder notifications (if enabled)

5. Generate & Download Rent Report

Clicks "Generate Report"

Sees PDF preview

Can download or copy a shareable link

6. Share with Landlord or Lender

Selects WhatsApp/email to send report

Uses auto-generated message or writes own

7. Optional: Request Landlord Verification

Sends 1-click verify link to landlord

Once landlord confirms, badge added to report
